The Story

happy Souls may be living in a land far away from the world as we know it, but that doesn'tmean they are unreachable. Happy Souls are known to join forces to create a positiveenvironment wherever they go - and on the blockchain, they truly bring optimism to the network,connecting like-minded individuals on a happy note with access to knowledge, exclusivity andmuch more.

The idea behind Happy Souls came about from the reality of life, whereby negative eventssometimes overtake our lives. Happy Souls is all about bringing positivity and happiness topeople. We are facing so many stressful situations in our everyday lives and this project aims toteach people how to cope with stress and bring more happiness into their day-to-day routine.

By creating a community of Happy Souls, we believe we can bring more positive vibes into thereal world ... through the virtual one. Become a happy soul by owning a Happy Soul NFT andlet's start creating more happiness in our own way.
